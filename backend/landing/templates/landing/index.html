How The App Works?

User Protection

Havenly prioritizes user safety by monitoring online interactions and identifying potential threats such as harassment and doxxing. The app uses AI to provide real-time alerts when suspicious activities are detected, allowing users to take immediate action to protect themselves.

Emotional Support

The app offers AI-powered psychological assistance, providing personalized coping strategies, mood tracking, and therapeutic exercises for women affected by online harassment. It identifies signs of emotional distress and offers timely guidance or professional help, promoting mental well-being.

Education and Awareness

Havenly empowers users with resources on online safety, including tutorials on privacy protection and recognizing digital threats. This feature encourages continuous learning about digital literacy, helping women stay informed and confident in navigating the digital world.

Data Management

The app helps users manage and secure their online accounts by promoting strong passwords, two-factor authentication, and monitoring data sharing. It educates users on recognizing phishing attempts and unauthorized access, ensuring comprehensive protection of personal information.
